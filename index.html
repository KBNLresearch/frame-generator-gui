Frame generator

GitHub About

The frame generator calculates the most important keywords for one or more Dutch text files, based on topic modeling techniques. It subsequently finds the words that are most strongly associated with each keyword, forming so-called frames.

Use the controls below to upload your documents and determine which part-of-speech tags are to be included, as well as in which direction of the keyword and to what distance related frame words are to be searched for.
